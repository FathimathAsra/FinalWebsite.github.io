POPULAR DESTINATIONS

KEYODHOO SHIPWRECK DIVE SITE

Lately of great interest and abuzz, it has become a very popular destination among the visitors and locals alike. Vibrant with the colors of life, you are sure to experience a world of life, that you have never seen before. The wreckage is located a few kilometers away from the island of V.Keyodhoo.

HISTORICAL MOSQUE

This historical mosque commonly known as the Hukuru Miskiyy, is a UNESCO protected world heritage site. It is one of the first ever mosques to be built in Maldives and it has been built with marbles which have been carved by hands of Maldivian craftsmen, which is why it has become so popular

BANANA REEF

The great Banana Reef is actually the first diving site discovered in Maldives. The banana shaped reef is micro world of its own, full of life. A very popular diving site amongst tourists, this is one of the best Maldivian experiences you can get while you are enjoying your life in Maldives.
