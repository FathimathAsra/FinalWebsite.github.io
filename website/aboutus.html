WHO ARE WE

We are a vibrant team of locals, who seek to provide a great service for tourists who visit our pearl of a nation. When tourism started in Maldives, we saw it as an opportunity for people like us to express ourselves and help promote our Island Nation.

Even though we started DvTours just a year ago, our team is very experienced in the tourism sector, most of us having worked at resorts in different jobs for different tenures. Our expertise comes from the fact that we have been dealing with tourists first hand, all our careers.

Maldives is one of the most beautiful countries in the world, and that has never been expressed enough. In part, it is due to the fact that resorts have been expensive for a lot of people along with he hassle of travelling a long distance. But with the introduction of local tourism, in the recent years, the tourism sector has seen a boom in inflow of tourists, from around the globe. This year, we hit 1.5 million tourist visitors to Maldives and the numbers are rising.

What we intend to do is something that we Maldives has yet to see. We provide a local experience for our guests, unlike predefined packages of predefined activities. Let your inner adventurous self go wild in Maldives and be spontaneous. And our main service is to provide you with the assurance that you will be taken care of in your most memorable moments of spontaneity.

Embark with us on a journey to experience Maldives, not just see it. We will help you experience the local life, where you will get the chance to mingle with locals and leed the Maldivian life. And we want to show you that you don't have to break the bank to enjoy the white sandy beaches, or the enjoyment and playfulness of the pristine waters.

We are DvTours
